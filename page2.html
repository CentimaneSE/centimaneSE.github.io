Our Story 

Our team met in a Cyber Security Leaders international exchange where ten representatives from the United Kingdom and ten from the United States worked collaboratively on cyber security challenges. In between delving into a plethora of topics in both classrooms and private institutions, a group of us got together and formulated the foundational ideas of an organization that would equalize representation and opportunity in STEM. As representatives for Cyber Security from our respective countries, our organization, Centimane, is inspired by the recognition of need and lack of opportunity across the globe. Our mission is to bridge the gap between underserved and advanced societies by disseminating material, curriculum, and resources to those without.

Our Mission

To supply resources and equal educational opportunity to underserved individuals.

Centimane's Pillars 

Diversity

Diversity to focus on equalizing opportunity and representation.

Academics

Academics to focus on the dissemination of materials and creation of comprehensive and intuitive cyber security curriculum for younger audiences.

Outreach

Outreach to broaden our impact beyond local area.
